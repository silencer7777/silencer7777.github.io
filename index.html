About Me

Hello. My name is Brett Cope.

I am a 28 year old male, born in 1996 in Apple Valley, CA.

I grew up loving nature and I always try my best to stay active.

At the age of 10, I hopped on a skateboard for the first time and immediately fell in love with the sport.

As I grew older, I explored a variety of different hobbies, from skateboarding to playing sports and eventually picked up painting as a recent hobby.

Every hobby I commit myself to helps me self-reflect and connect with people.

I love and care for people and I try to put others first before myself. Being a family man is valuable for me and I cherish every relationship in my life. Having a strong will to learn and to progress is shown through my character and is affirmed through what others say about me.

Introduction

Let me get right into the purpose of this autobiography! My goal is to foster vulnerability, enhance professional networking and continuously expand my knowledge with the ultimate aim of becoming a professional full-stack web developer.

Achievements

I completed the 2017 Los Angeles Marathon, been certified in the past to operate heavy machinery like forklifts and skidsteers and other heavy equipment. I lived in two different apartments in my past and am hoping to become a homeowner in the next few years. These accomplishments demonstrate my commitment to personal growth and self-improvement, with the ultimate goal of becoming a professional full-stack web developer.

Future Goals

My future goals include becoming a full-stack web developer, finishing college, acquiring a vehicle and starting a family. These objectives demonstrate my commitment to professional growth, personal wellness, and practical life improvements. By pursuing these goals, I aim to create a stable and successful future for myself.
