My Journey

I am currently in a discipleship program called Emmaus, located in Compton, CA.

Since being here, I have maintained my sobriety, gotten closer to God and I have become a stronger, more confident individual.

I can't begin to express how grateful I am for the URM and all the people who have supported me. My biggest thanks go to all who have helped.

I have also gained some form of peace so I don't plan to stop making progress and will continue to move forward.

Column 1: Introduction

I'll get right into the purpose of this autobiography. My goal is to foster vulnerability, enhance professional networking, and continuously expand my knowledge with the ultimate aim of becoming a professional full-stack web developer.

Column 2: Achievements

Some achievements include completing the 2017 Los Angeles Marathon, approaching one year of sobriety and developing this website. These accomplishments demonstrate my commitment to personal growth and self-improvement, with the ultimate goal of becoming a professional full-stack web developer.

My future goals include becoming a full-stack web developer, maintaining my sobriety, and starting and supporting a family of my own someday. These objectives demonstrate my commitment to professional growth, personal wellness and practical life improvements. By pursuing these goals, I aim to create a stable and successful future for myself.
