About Me

Hello. My name is Brett Cope.

I am a 28 year old male who was born in 1996 in Apple Valley, CA.

I grew up loving nature and being active.

At the age of 10, I hopped on a skateboard for the first time and immediately fell in love with the sport.

As I grew older, I explored a variety of different hobbies, from skateboarding to playing sports and eventually picked up painting as a recent hobby.

Every hobby I commit myself to helps me self-reflect and connect with individuals.

I love and care for people, and I try to put others first before myself.

Romans 12:10 - Be devoted to one another in love. Honor one another above yourselves.
