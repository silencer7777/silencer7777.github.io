About Me

Hello. My name is Brett Cope.

I am a 28-year-old male, born in 1996 in Apple Valley, CA.

I grew up loving nature, and I always try my best to stay active.

At the age of 10, I hopped on a skateboard for the first time and immediately fell in love with the sport.

As I grew older, I explored a variety of hobbies—from skateboarding to playing sports—and recently, I picked up painting.

Every hobby I commit to helps me self-reflect and connect with people.

I love and care for others, and I try to put them before myself. Being a family man is important to me, and I cherish every relationship in my life. My strong will to learn and progress is reflected in my character and affirmed by what others say about me.

Romans 12:10 - Be devoted to one another in love. Honor one another above yourselves.
